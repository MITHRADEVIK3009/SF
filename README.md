## üí° Final Advice for Absolute Beginners

1. **Don't panic** - The exam is passable with focused study
2. **Cert Prep is your bible** - Complete it thoroughly
3. **Practice questions reveal patterns** - Take at least 120-180 practice questions
4. **Read explanations** - Even for correct answers
5. **Scenario-based questions** - Think "What would Salesforce recommend?"
6. **Use elimination** - Often 1 answer is clearly wrong
7. **Time management** - 105 min = ~1.75 min per question
8. **Trust your prep** - If you did the work, you'll pass
